Motivation

Vestivise is for anyone stuck with improper allocations or forced into paying unnecessarily high fees just so they may retire comfortably. Intrigued by investing as a high schooler, I asked my parents to share more about their investments. Much to my dismay, neither one understood anything about their portfolios. I decided to take a look at the quarterly reports given to them by their asset managers. I read through the many pages of small printed text and unhelpful black and white graphs. I knew exactly what I was looking for, yet I couldn’t even uncover some of the most fundamental information. Vestivise is the solution to a status quo that continually leaves investors feeling confused. We help people understand their investments regardless of their financial knowledge. Every time you view your dashboard, you will have a clear understanding of your portfolio.

Josh Gelinas
CEO & Cofounder
