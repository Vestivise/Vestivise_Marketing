Security

Vestivise is a secure platform to understand your investments and does not internally store sensitive information such as your bank account login information, account numbers, or passwords. User financial information is securely retrieved when you log on using industry-standard encryption techniques.

Internally, Vestivise uses cryptographic signing to verify the authenticity and integrity of requests to our servers. Transport layer security is additionally implemented on all of our web pages to ensure your privacy is protected and to prevent third party interception or manipulation of data.
